NPUB Outlier Discovery — Anti-Phishing Fingerprint Scanner

No predefined pattern. Scanner finds the most anomalous section automatically. Z-score = statistical surprise.

How It Works

1. Scan NPUB at 13 window sizes (3..20 chars) × all positions
2. For each window, compute Shannon entropy, compare to baseline distribution (20k random NPUBs)
3. Z-score = how many std-devs below expected entropy. Max z-score = the NPUB's "fingerprint anchor"
4. Browser highlights the anchor section. User learns to recognize it.

Key insight
Finding ANY extreme outlier: ~10k tries (seconds on laptop)

Matching a SPECIFIC outlier pattern: millions of times harder

The z-score IS the proof-of-work. Higher z = more work = stronger visual fingerprint = harder to forge.

Which scales produce outliers?

Outliers found at all scales. Short windows (3-6 chars) produce 45% of outliers — even "hhh" is statistically surprising.
