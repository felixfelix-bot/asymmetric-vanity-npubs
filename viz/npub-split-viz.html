SPLIT APPROACH: Vanity Zone + Anti-Phish Zone

Two independent sections with DIFFERENT char palettes. Vanity is readable, anti-phish is unforgeable. Visual contrast is free.

The Concept

GREEN ZONE = vanity name "meshmate" — uses chars {m,e,s,h,a,t}
ORANGE ZONE = anti-phish fingerprint — uses ONLY other chars {q,p,z,r,y,...}

Two completely different color palettes. Human eye separates them instantly.
Attacker must match BOTH zones simultaneously: exponentially harder than either alone.

Grinding Strategy: Sequential, Not Simultaneous

Step 1: Grind for "meshmate" prefix (~2^37 keys, few hours on laptop)
Step 2: Among those keys, run outlier scanner on each
Step 3: Pick the key with strongest outlier in complement charset

The outlier is DISCOVERED, not constrained. Alice accepts millions of different valid patterns. Attacker must replicate ONE. That's the asymmetry.

Total cost: ~2^37 (just the vanity grind). Outlier is free.
Attacker cost: must match exact outlier = astronomically higher.
